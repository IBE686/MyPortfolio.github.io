Featured Projects

Project 1: Armed and Dangerous

The inspiration for this design came early 2025. "Armed and Dangerous” is a bold conceptual design that visually communicates strength, readiness, and the fiery determination needed to pursue one’s ideals. The central figure — a mysterious, hooded warrior on horseback — symbolizes courage and inner power, while the dark, misty atmosphere and blazing lower tones reflect the battle between fear and ambition

Project 2: Sampoto Air

Sampoto Airline is a visually effective promotion for travel. It uses a clean composition where the blue airplane and sky establish trust and the product. The smiling family with matching blue luggage appeals to leisure travelers. The design quickly communicates the brand and its commitment to speed and value/safety.

Project 3: JoeLight

This design for Joelight, an event lighting brand, captures the energy, color, and vibrance they bring to every occasion. The bold typography, red-white contrast, and blue lighting effects reflect the intensity and creative atmosphere of stage lights cutting through smoke — symbolizing Joelight’s dynamic presence in every event.
